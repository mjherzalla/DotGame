Wellcome to DotsGame

Click dots and have fun

In the game, dots move from the top to the bottom of the screen.
A player tries to click on the dots, and receives points when they are successful.


How to play

The has 25 levels, every level the player has collect 25 dots under 40 second to pass to the next level.
The scoring depends on the size of the dot and the current speed selected by the user.
The game become harder every level since the minimum speed of the dots increases.
